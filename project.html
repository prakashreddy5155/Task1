Processor Basic Details:

845 is a high-performance 64-bit ARM LTE system on a chip designed by Qualcomm and introduced in late 2017.
Fabricated on Samsung's 10nm 10LPP, the 845 features four Kryo 385 Silver high-efficiency cores operating at 1.7 GHz,
along with four high-performance Kryo 385 Gold cores operating at 2.8 GHz
